Investigation questions

Use these to structure your work. They are deliberately broad — you decide which evidence answers each one. Your graded deliverable is the report, not these answers.

  1. The application email. Is its stated origin consistent with the claimed identity and location? [email headers]
  2. The profile photo. Is it authentic and original to the candidate? [reverse image search · metadata]
  3. The username / identity trail. Does everything using this name belong to one consistent, real person? [username correlation]
  4. The social & professional footprint. What does the shape and age of his accounts tell you? [behavioural · cross-platform]
  5. His own claims vs. the public record. Do the CV and the content he posts hold up? [search · geolocation]

Deliverable — Intelligence Report

Produce a short intelligence report (not just answers) containing:

  • Your findings, each with supporting evidence (screenshots).
  • A confidence level for every finding — Low Medium High — with reasoning.
  • An overall verdict: is the identity genuine or misleading?
  • A short OPSEC note — the precautions you took during the investigation.

A report template is provided separately by your instructor (report_template.md).

Confidence reminder: one matching detail is a coincidence, not a confirmation. Rate High only when independent sources agree.
