About

Where Greece's security community meets

Now in its fourth year, the Forum is a vendor-neutral, community-run event. Expect deep technical talks, hands-on villages and a lot of hallway conversations.

Track 1

Threat Intelligence

Tracking adversaries, intelligence-led defence and the practical side of attribution. Includes the flagship threat-intel panel.

Track 2

Offensive Security

Red teaming, exploit development and the latest from the assessment trenches — straight from the people doing the work.

Track 3

Privacy & Compliance

GDPR in practice, data-protection engineering and the regulatory landscape for Greek and EU organisations.

🎓 Student track

Discounted student passes and a dedicated networking corner. A large contingent comes from Athens universities every year.

🛠️ Hands-on villages

Lock-picking, OSINT and a small CTF run across both days in the Zappeion's east wing.

🤝 Community-run

Organised by volunteers from the Greek security community. All profits go back into next year's edition.
