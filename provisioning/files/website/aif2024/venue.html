Getting there

Zappeion Megaron

The Forum returns to the Zappeion — the historic exhibition hall in the National Gardens, right in the centre of Athens.

🗺️ Zappeion Megaron · National Gardens, Athens

The Zappeion sits between Syntagma and the Panathenaic Stadium, on the edge of the National Gardens. The main entrance faces Vasilissis Olgas Avenue.

  • Address Zappeion Megaron, Vasilissis Olgas Ave, Athens 105 57
  • Dates Wednesday 6 – Thursday 7 November 2024
  • Doors 08:30 both days
  • Metro Syntagma (Lines 2 & 3) or Akropoli (Line 2), ~8 min walk
  • Accessibility Step-free access via the east entrance; all halls on one level
  • Cloakroom Available in the main foyer, free for pass holders

🚇 By metro

Alight at Syntagma and walk south through the National Gardens, or at Akropoli and head east. Both are under 10 minutes on foot.

🚌 By bus / trolley

Several lines stop on Vasilissis Olgas and Amalias avenues, directly outside the gardens.

🅿️ Parking

Limited paid parking near the Panathenaic Stadium. We strongly recommend public transport.
