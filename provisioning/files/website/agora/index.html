Is Exarcheia actually safe to live in? [r/athens]
Moving to Athens for an MSc and considering Exarcheia for the rent prices. What's it actually like day-to-day?
Exarcheia gets a bad rep online but honestly it's one of the more genuine neighborhoods in the city. Been here almost two years and the community feel is unlike anywhere else — know most of the people in my building, local cafés have regulars, it's quite alive. The noise can be a thing on weekends but you get used to it. Rent is decent for how central it is too.
